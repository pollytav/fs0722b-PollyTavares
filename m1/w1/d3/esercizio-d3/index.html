Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart, cranberries, and creamy brie.It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Featured in:

Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin oil

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubs
  • 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

Heat oven to 400°F. Transfer the dough to a large parchment-covered baking sheet (or press it into a 9 x 13-inch baking dish). Use your fingers to poke deep dents — like seriously, poke all the way down until you touch the baking sheet — all over the surface of the dough. Then drizzle a tablespoon or two of olive oil evenly all over the top of the dough, and sprinkle evenly with the fresh rosemary needles and sea salt.(...)
